# CareerCompas — Find Your Path After Graduation

Not sure what to do next after graduation? CareerCompas helps you discover meaningful career directions based on your strengths, interests, and goals.

- **Who it's for:** Graduates, soon-to-be graduates, and career-curious people unsure which path to take.
- **What it does:** Matches your profile to careers, suggests learning steps, and outlines realistic next moves.
- **Why it helps:** Turns confusion into a clear, confident plan — fast.

Ready to turn uncertainty into opportunity? Explore CareerCompas and take the first step toward a career you actually want.
